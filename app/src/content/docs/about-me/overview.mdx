## About This Garden

This is a personal technical garden where I explore, document, and share my journey through modern software development. Like a garden that grows over time, this site continuously evolves with new insights, experiences, and learnings.

Here, you'll find my explorations in:

- **Cloud Architecture**: Serverless applications, Google Cloud Platform, Infrastructure as Code, DevOps, Identifier
- **Developer Experience**: Tools and practices that make development more enjoyable and productive
- **AI & Modern Development**: Genkit, Firebase, and emerging technologies
- **Software Craftsmanship**: Design patterns, DDD, clean architecture, and quality practices

## About Nozomi Koborinai

I graduated from an IT technical college in March 2019, where I built my foundation in object-oriented programming with Java.

Over my 6 years of experience, the first four were spent developing electronic medical record systems using C#, teaching at Flutter University (Japan's Flutter engineering community), and co-authoring books on Flutter design patterns. Currently, I work at a Google Cloud Partner company in Japan, specializing in **Serverless Application development** (Google Cloud, Firebase, DevOps, IaC, Identifier). My deep interest in software architecture has made me proficient in ensuring quality through DDD and clean architecture principles.

## Why koborin.ai?

The name **koborin.ai** carries a meaningful play on words:

- **koborinai** - My family name, representing my personal identity
- **koborin.ai** - The domain combining my nickname with the `.ai` TLD, symbolizing the intersection of personal journey and technology
- **AI Explorer** - Reflecting my dedication to exploring and contributing to AI-powered products and frameworks like Genkit

This dual meaning captures both who I am and what I'm passionate about: bridging traditional software craftsmanship with cutting-edge AI technologies, always with a focus on exceptional developer experience.
